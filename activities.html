Code

Throughout these two weeks, we immerse ourselves in the world of code and learn Ruby, HTML, and CSS. By the end of these two weeks, you will be able to code a calculator, websites, and even games!

group

Games

It might seem weird at first, but playing icebreaker and other fun kinds of games 2-3 times a day really helps bond the camp family. The games are really fun and innovative. Yoga is also another fun activity we do at camp.

settings

Food

Food is catered from a different restaurant every day- you are bound to grow your food palette within these two weeks. The food is RIDICULOUSLY good, from tacos to mediterranean wraps. The snacks are also A+, you'll never go hungry.

The teachers and TAs!
All of the teachers and TAs are extremely helpful, and always willing to sit down and work with you on any struggles you are having. Besides helping with problems, they teach the lectures well and go at a pace that allows everyone to catch up and understand where they are. Overall, the teachers and TAs are influential role models that all the campers look up to and admire.

Karlie Kloss

Karlie came to visit us in Week One on Wednesday and Thursday! She had meaningful conversations with us, and helped us with coding. She's a great role model, and we want to thank her for giving us this once in a lifetime opportunity! Thank you Karlie!

Who are we?

We are Laura, Nayeli, and Aarushi, 3 Kode With Klossy 2017 scholars from Austin, TX. We are all super excited for the rest of the camps this summer, and we hope this website helped you!
