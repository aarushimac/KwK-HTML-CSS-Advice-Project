Innovation

Using coding, we learn how to create products that are real world applicable. We are able to bring our ideas to life by using Ruby, HTML, and CSS. By the end of the two weeks, we will be able to create websites and other skill-application products.

group

Collaboration

We learn how to work with other women and empower each other to pursue creation and coding as a passion or even onwards to a career. Problem solving skills, encouragement teams, and friendships are developed during these two weeks.

settings

Explanation

In this environment, teachers, TAs, and other students are key to explanation. Because of this, Kode with Klossy has a reputable atmosphere of being positive and life-changing. By the end of these two weeks, you will not just be a class, but a family.

What can you expect out of Kode with Klossy?
You might be scared at first, but diving into the process, hands-on, is extremely rewarding. Through Kode with Klossy, you will not just exit with coding skills, but a family you can rely on beyond just the two-week camp. At the end of Kode with Klossy, you will have a family, new coding knowledge, and teamwork skills you can learn nowhere else.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

Kode with Klossy empowers girls to learn techniques that are not usually exposed to them. This helps them form networks to help each other and become more self-confident. Furthermore, they are able to apply their skills for the future to execute their vision. Overall, Kode with Klossy scholars are better prepared for the workforce and better prepared to fulfill their dreams as change makers.

Who are we?

We are Laura, Nayeli, and Aarushi, 3 Kode With Klossy 2017 scholars from Austin, TX. We are all super excited for the rest of the camps this summer, and we hope this website helped you!
